Frequently Asked Questions

How do I recover my Coinbase account?

To recover your account, click on "Forgot Password" on the login page. You will receive an email with instructions to reset your password. If you have 2FA enabled, you may need to verify your identity.

Why is my transaction pending?

Transactions may be pending due to network congestion, low gas fees, or security verification. Most transactions complete within 30 minutes. If it takes longer, please contact our support team.

How do I enable two-factor authentication?

Go to Settings > Security > Two-Factor Authentication. You can use an authenticator app or SMS verification. We recommend using an authenticator app for enhanced security.

What cryptocurrencies does Coinbase support?

Coinbase supports hundreds of cryptocurrencies including Bitcoin, Ethereum, Litecoin, and many more. Visit our assets page for the complete list of supported cryptocurrencies.

How do I contact Coinbase support?

Click the "Get Support" button above to connect with our 24/7 support team. You can also access help through the Coinbase app or visit our Help Center for self-service options.
